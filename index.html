About Us

Hello, welcome to Raj's Cafe. Here, we serve some snacks, drinks, and more! We offer top quality service with 5 stars on 'Yelp'. If you are hungry or even thirsty, make sure to drop by!

Our address is located at, 123 Oven Road, 99806 WA

Raj's Cafe was founded in 2025 by Raj Sukumaran. We offer some delicious food and snacks that you can enjoy to your liking. Some food we offer is, Chai, Coffee, Soft Drinks, Pastries, and a few Breakfast items. All of our ingredients are extremely fresh and naturally sourced which makes it delicious to perfection. Cant come to our Cafe? No problem! We also deliver on Uber Eats, Doordash, and Cafe-Go! We want to make sure that you have the best experience possible in order to make this a relaxing, yet fun place for you. We have a large dining place with lots of room, so that you can have parties of 10 or less! Please give us a review on Yelp, or our website if you want to! Have fun, and make sure to stop by!
